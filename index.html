Optimizing Cooling for AI Data Centers 10/2025 – 11/2025
Problem: High energy consumption in data centers due to inefficient cooling under varying loads and temperatures.
Data: 3,498 hourly data center records, 8 core features (load, ambient temp, power consumption, etc.).
Approach: Equal‑frequency binning + one‑hot encoding; Apriori algorithm (support 0.15, confidence 0.7, lift 1.1); 10‑fold cross‑validation.
Outcome/Impact: Identified 18 strong rules: high load + high ambient temp → high power (lift=2.73, conf=91%). Proposed "dynamic free cooling" strategy, estimated 10% cooling energy reduction.
My contribution: Built the environment‑energy dataset, performed discretization/encoding, implemented Apriori mining, and conducted statistical validation (Pandas/NumPy).
